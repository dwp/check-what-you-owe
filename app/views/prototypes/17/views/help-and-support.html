Help and support

We aim to help you set up a sustainable way to repay the money you owe to the Department for Work and Pensions. This information is designed to support you if you need help with your debt.

If you’re struggling to pay

Contact Debt Management immediately if you think you might miss a payment. You can call us to ask for a payment holiday, change the amount you pay each month or to ask for help and advice.

Telephone: 0800 916 0614
Textphone: 0345 604 6697
Monday to Friday 8am to 7.30pm
Saturday 9am to 4pm

If you’d like independent advice

For help and advice on dealing with debt, budgeting and more, contact:

  • Citizens Advice
  • StepChange
  • Money Advice Service

If your circumstances have changed

Call Debt Management if you want to make extra payments to pay off the debt more quickly, or to tell us about changes to your account.
