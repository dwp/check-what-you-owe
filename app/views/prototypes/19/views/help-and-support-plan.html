Help and support

We want you to pay back the money you owe at a rate you can afford. If you need help to manage your money, you can use the information here to get practical support and advice.

If you’re struggling to pay

Contact our debt management team immediately if you think you might miss a payment. You can ask for a payment holiday, change the amount you pay each month, or get help and advice.

If you’d like independent advice

For help and advice on dealing with debt, budgeting and more, contact:

  • Citizens Advice
  • StepChange Debt Charity
  • Money Advice Service

If your circumstances have changed

Contact our debt management team if you want to make extra payments to pay off the debt quicker, or to tell us about changes to your account.
